Island Protector 2

  • Originality: The game is similar to any Top Flight Shooter Games.
  • Technical Merit: The game features arrow keys and space bar for actions. Space ships move according to their defined physics. Ability to earn points when blowing up an enemy. Ability to die if one gets touched by a ship.
  • Prototype Postmortem: After going through a tutorial to build this game, I managed to understand the games mechanics, and was able to modify them to make it a custom game. Hardest part was creating the Menu screen, as I have never done so.

  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Any Top Flight Shooter Games are similar, but this game is based off Luis Zuno game.
  • High Concept: Survive and shoot enemy ships in order to gain points.
  • Theme: Don't get your character killed.
  • Prototype Goal: Shooting, moving around the map.
  • Player Experience Goals: To have fun shooting down alien ships, and try to survive.
  • Gameplay: The player can use the arrow keys to move (UP | DOWN | LEFT | RIGHT) and use the SPACEBAR key to shoot enemies. They can collect power-ups that increase their velocity. If they player collides with an enemy ship, they will blow up and begin to respawn. When respawning, no enemy can hit you. When you get hit, or an enemy gets hit from your bullets, an explosion animation goes off.
  • Strategies: Dodge and weave enemies coming at you. Blow them up when they're at a good distance to shoot.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: You the Player, is the Island Protector in the planet full of islands. Alien ships have invaded your planet and plan to blow up these islands because they're jealous that you have a planet full of islands. You must protect your planet and shoot down the infinite attack from the aliens.
  • Target Audience: Anyone can play this game.
  • Play Time: Infinite time.
