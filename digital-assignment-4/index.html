Chicken Hunt

  • Originality: Chicken Hunt is a game for skilled players who can achieve a high score if they know when to shoot.
  • Technical Merit: The physics of the spinning player as well as the physics for the chickens riding along the fence.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I learned about rotational phycis, specifically how to manipulate how the character and hits shots all flow together. Hardest part was making the physics for the chicken to follow around.
  • Prototype Assets:
    • All art is handmade by me using 'Paint 3D'
    • All sounds are borrowed from freesfx.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game:
    • The code for this example is adapted from the Design on 3D rotation.
  • High Concept: A spinner shooter where timing shots achieve for a high-score.
  • Theme: Chicken
  • Mandated Variety: Simple shooter by clicking. Rotational physics.
  • Prototype Goal: The goal was to achieve a shooter with 3D rotation.
  • Player Experience Goals: Having fun killing chickens.
  • Gameplay: Click to shoot the chickens, pretty simple.
  • Strategies: Timing the shots to hit the chickens.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: You're just a simple farmer, on your farm, and one day all the chickens began to troll you and cluck all day at you, and so you decide to kill them by throwing their own eggs at them.
  • Target Audience: Anyone can play!
  • Play Time: 1 Minute gameplay.
