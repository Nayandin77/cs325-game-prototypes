  • Technical Merit: The game features 5 different playable characters that can be swapped at anytime during the game. Each character is able to use 2 special ability attacks.

  • High Concept: Top Down Shooter that allows you to swap in game characters and use special attacks.
  • Theme: Top Down Shooter
  • Mandated Variety: Mouse, Keyboard, deterministic, Achiever, rpg-shooter
  • Prototype Goal: Switching states between characters.
  • Player Experience Goals: Have fun figuring out what characters do what damage type.
  • Gameplay: WASD - to move around, Mouse Pointer - to aim where to shoot, Mouse Click - to shoot, Q & E to use special abilities, R - to reload.
  • Strategies: Swap between characters to figure out how to damage the enemy.
